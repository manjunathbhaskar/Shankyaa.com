SECTION 2 OF 8 · PROBLEM
The Problem

Two unsolved problems at internet scale

A
Identity — Broken & Expensive
▶ $50B+ lost annually to identity fraud across banking, KYC, and digital access
▶ Face-swap deepfakes are undetectable by current systems — 2024 saw 10x increase in deepfake fraud
▶ KYC is slow, expensive, breachable — $2B+ in biometric data breach costs per year
▶ Every system stores biometric data — a honeypot for attackers
B
Video Storage — Wasteful & Untrustworthy
▶ $25B+/yr CDN spend growing 30% YoY — storing and transmitting redundant pixels
▶ H.264 was designed in 2003 for broadcast TV — it has no semantic understanding
▶ 90%+ of video data is background, texture, repeated frames — none of it is meaning
▶ No authenticity layer — any frame can be replaced, any face swapped, zero detection

Root cause: Both are unsolvable with existing technology. No codec has authenticity built in. No biometric system works without storing data. Existing solutions are band-aids on broken foundations.

BONUS · IP / PATENTS
Intellectual Property

2 patents pending — 4 novel contributions

1 NOVEL
AWSP — Attention-Weighted Semantic Prioritisation

Tracks implicit viewer signals (pause=1.0, rewind=0.9, zoom=0.8, dwell=0.7, skip=−0.4) with 5-min temporal decay. Allocates reconstruction compute budget proportionally to high-interest semantic anchors. No prior work applies implicit attention to video compression quality allocation.

Patent A — Lumisiriuss core
2 NOVEL
SSPC — Semantic-to-Swarm Protocol with Commitment

Every P2P fragment carries SHA-256(fragment + Merkle_root + salt) + HMAC-SHA256 signature. Peers verify before caching. No peer can forge or alter a fragment without breaking the commitment. Provenance traceable to origin encoder node.

Patent B — P2P layer
3 NOVEL
DARB — Device-Aware Reconstruction Budget

Real-time device profiling (CPU cores, GPU VRAM, RAM) maps to tiered reconstruction: 4K·60fps Ultra → 1080p·60fps High → 720p·30fps Standard → 480p·24fps Lite. Maximises quality within hardware constraints without user configuration.

Patent B — Reconstruction layer
4 NOVEL
RBCP — Relay-Based Contribution Protocol

Credit multiplier: 1.0 + 0.24 × min(relay_ratio, 1.0). Nodes that relay fragments earn higher reconstruction quality. Self-organising P2P incentive without tokens or blockchain. relay_ratio = fragments_relayed / fragments_consumed.

Patent B — P2P economics
